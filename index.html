üî• Popular Questions

How do I know if a part is genuine?

Look for verified sellers, check part numbers, and ask for authenticity certificates. Our AI system also helps verify genuine parts.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, mobile money (Opay, PalmPay), and cash on delivery for verified sellers.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery typically takes 1-3 business days within the same city, and 3-7 days for inter-state deliveries.

Can I return a part if it doesn't fit?

Yes, you can return unused parts within 7 days if they don't match the description or fit your vehicle.
